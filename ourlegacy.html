Our Legacy

St. Wilfred’s Group of Colleges is one of the premier academic institutions in North India.. Students from all streams are chiseled here and excel in the chosen areas of their interest. Established with the vision to instill a sense of responsibility among the students to participate in citizenship duties and strive towards common welfare and to nurture overall development of the students through imparting quality education.The society takes pride in being a cohesive group where a conscious and thoughtful response is given to critical need for excellent and relevant education in a innovative manner.

The group is also felicitated with the Education Excellence Award-Best Private Educational Institute of 2013 for providing best services in the field of academics and education. St.Wilfred’s College is awarded third position in the Top ten lists of the best co-educational private institutional of 2014 by India Today Magazine in a Survey conducted by ‘Nielsen’. In 2015 our group has been ranked 21th & 24th in the best colleges of India by Nielsen survey (India Today). In 2017, a new feather added in a cap, the group has been awarded 16th Rank in top 40 colleges of India. For providing quality education our group has recently been awarded Overall Excellence Award 2017 by Shri Vasudev Devnani

Since its inception, the group has widened its horizon and is running various professional, post graduate, law, education & engineering colleges as well. The first campus was established at Mansarovar, Jaipur. With the passage of time the popularity of SWC has raised manifold and has gradually established Institutions in and outside Rajasthan.

Today, it is one of the most distinguished educational groups which has number of qualities to account for. The group has a team of well qualified and experienced faculty members and a very efficient supportive staff. The colleges’ offer a number of courses in science, commerce, management, humanities, law, engineering, mass communication, I.T., Computers and so on.

The well stacked e-Library, well equipped laboratories, NSS units, gymnasiums, hostel facilities, cafeterias, swimming pool, sports facilities and art of state infrastructures are some of the salient features.
