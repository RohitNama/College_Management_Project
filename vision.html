Vision & Mission & Values

Vision

  1. St. Wilfred’s is a conscious and thoughtful response to a critical need for excellent and relevant education in a traditional, innovative and creative frame work.
  2. We take pride in being a cohesive group who shares the fundamental aims where the staff specialise in excellence and high standards of achievements.

Mission

  1. To cater the multiple abilities & intelligence present in learners for realisation of their true potential and individual needs of developments.
  2. To in still among the students a sense of responsibilities to participate in citizenship duties and strive towards common welfare.
  3. To nurture overall development of the students through enrichment and quality education.
  4. To promote involvement of students with community outside the college and other social concerns through community service program.

Values

  1. My beliefs run through everything I do. My beliefs, my values are my anchor and when people try to drag me, as I know they will, it is to that sense of right or wrong, that sense of who I am and what I believe, to which I will always hold.

St.Wilfred Pg Collage

St. Wilfred’s Group of Colleges is one of the premier academic institutions in North India.. Students from all streams are chiselled here and excel in the chosen areas of their interest. Established with the vision to instill a sense of responsibility among the students to participate in citizenship duties and strive towards common welfare and to nurture overall development of the students through imparting quality education.
